About Us

we are a dedicated team of neuro physiotherapists, engineers, and innovators committed to revolutionizing hand paralysis rehabilitation. Our mission is to integrate cutting-edge hardware and intelligent software to provide real-time monitoring and effective therapy solutions for individuals recovering from hand paralysis.

  • AI-powered hardware and software for precise hand paralysis monitoring and rehabilitation.
  • Developed by neuro physiotherapists and engineers for scientifically backed recovery solutions.
  • User-friendly, adaptive therapy tools with real-time progress tracking and personalized rehab plans.

We believe in combining science, technology, and compassionate care to restore independence and improve the quality of life for individuals affected by hand paralysis.
